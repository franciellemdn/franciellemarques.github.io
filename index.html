Intro

Why Technology?

My immense desire to delve into the world of technology and discover its particularities and characteristics that makes it such a necessary and life-transforming medium.

At 8 years old, I was fascinated by computers. My dream was to take a basic course and get a computer so that I could learn about the world of technology, which for me was limited to the computer. I didn’t really know what technology was or even the power of transformation it possessed.

I was 12 years old when two dreams came true just as I’d hoped. The first was attending a basic course in computer science which I achieved through a social project I participated in. The second, a computer, which I received from an aunt. It was with this computer that I was able to unveil some of the mysteries of technology. I learned the basic things in the course and other functions I learned the best way, through curiosity — hands on Experience.

At the Age of 14, I participated in the selection process of the IFG (Instituto Federal de Ciência e Tecnologia de Goiás) to compete for a place in the technical course in computer science. Little did I know that I would fall even more in love with technology.

And that’s what happened. I went through the selection process and got to know the real meaning of technology— its breadth and its importance in the world. I discovered that the polished stones of the neolithic period were technology & that the invention of agriculture was technology. I discovered, over all else, that living is technology because technology gives man innovations that change his trajectory, his way of understanding the world, and his perspective on life. Most importantly, I discovered that technology is my place in the world. It is what makes me good. It is what I seek to learn daily. It is what I dedicate myself to in the present to make technology my future.

Now, I’m a computer science graduate and I’m working towards my master’s for the same. I’m always looking to improve in my area (I can call it this after so many years spent together) And I’m loving every bit of it, overcoming the daily difficulties/challenges, and every day learning more and more how important technology is for my life.
